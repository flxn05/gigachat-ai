About

Via the gigaCHAT AI web interface, you can directly communicate with an AI Chatbot. Our application aims to give you more control over your data by using private AI models. This guarantees you more privacy and control over your data.



What we work with

gigaCHAT AI runs on a powerful Large Language Model (LLM) called Llama 3.2 uncensored, developed by Meta.
It runs entirely on our servers, i.e. no third party service can access its data.



How we manage your data and take responsibility

As mentioned, no third party service can access data sent to our servers. However, it may be possible that our systems get leaked, etc.

We are not responsible for any data leaks, information generated by the AI or similar. It is up to you to decide if you want to use our service.
